One of the best memories I experienced in my life

First of all let me tell you all that curently I'm pursuing my education in India. Last year on October 13th I've visited one of the beautiful districts in Assam called Karimganj. It was the day of Astami. I'm telling you a interesting fact. Karimganj stands on the river Kushiyara. Interestingly the other side of that river is my birth place. It is situated in Bangladesh called Zakiganj. Technically it's a border area. From my childhood I cherished to see my place from the other side of that river but couldn't. Finally that moment took place on 13th October, 2021. It seemed like a dream for me which was in real. To keep that moment restore in my life diary I took some snaps infront of the river Kushiyara. Overall the day was awesome and made a significant impact on my life.
